# Account Deletion Request - TourAssist Pakistan

At **TourAssist Pakistan**, we respect your privacy and provide you with full control over your personal data.

## How to Request Account Deletion
To delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

1.  Send an email to: **businessapptravel@gmail.com**
2.  Use the Subject Line: **Account Deletion Request - [Your Username/Email]**
3.  In the body of the email, please confirm that you wish to delete your account permanently.

Our team will process your request within **7 business days**. Once processed, you will receive a confirmation email.

## What Data Will Be Deleted?
Upon processing your request, the following data will be permanently removed from our active databases:
*   **User Profile**: Your name, email address, and profile picture.
*   **Saved Itineraries**: Any custom trips or saved destinations.
*   **Location History**: Any stored preferences related to your travel locations.
*   **AI Chat History**: Records of your interactions with our travel assistant.

## What Data is Retained?
We only retain data that we are legally required to keep or that is necessary for legitimate business security:
*   **Transaction Records**: If applicable, records of financial transactions are kept for 5 years for tax and audit purposes.
*   **Log Data**: Technical logs (stripped of personal identifiers) may be kept for a limited period to troubleshoot system issues.

## Important Note
Deleting your account is **permanent**. Once the process is complete, your data cannot be recovered.
